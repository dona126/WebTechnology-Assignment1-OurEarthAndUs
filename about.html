Resource Recovery

Each year, over 160 million tons of valuable resources are either buried in landfills or incinerated, both of which emit greenhouse gasses as a byproduct. Our goal is to transform waste into assets that will reduce :

  • Disposal costs
  • Loss of many valuable materials
  • Release of millions of tons of greenhouse gases that cause climate change
